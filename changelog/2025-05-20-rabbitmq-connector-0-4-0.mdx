[Conduit RabbitMQ Connector v0.4.0](https://github.com/conduitio-labs/conduit-connector-rabbitmq/releases/tag/v0.4.0) is now available, introducing new features and improvements to enhance functionality and reliability.

<!--truncate-->

### Key Highlights

- [**Add settings for skipping queue and exchange declare call**](https://github.com/conduitio-labs/conduit-connector-rabbitmq/pull/72). You can now configure the connector to skip declaring queues and exchanges, providing more flexibility for advanced RabbitMQ setups by [@rauanmayemir](https://github.com/rauanmayemir).
- [**Implement publisher confirmations**](https://github.com/conduitio-labs/conduit-connector-rabbitmq/pull/75). Added support for publisher confirmations, improving message delivery reliability by [@alarbada](https://github.com/alarbada).

If you have any questions, or more feedback feel free to join our [Discord Community](https://discord.meroxa.com/).

:::tip
For detailed setup and configuration instructions, refer to our [documentation](/docs/using/connectors/list/rabbitmq).
:::
