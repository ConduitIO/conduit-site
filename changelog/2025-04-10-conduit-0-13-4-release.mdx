[Conduit v0.13.4](https://github.com/ConduitIO/conduit/releases/tag/v0.13.4) is out and comes with a couple of configuration fixes.

<!--truncate-->

### More information

v0.13.4 comes with two fixes for configuration parameters that contain a dash
(`-`) in their names. The fixes are:

- [Bug: Certain environment variables are not respected](https://github.com/ConduitIO/conduit/issues/2206)
- [Fix configuration for the Postgres connection string](https://github.com/ConduitIO/conduit/pull/2244)

Thanks to one of our community members that let us know about the Postgres
connection string bug!
